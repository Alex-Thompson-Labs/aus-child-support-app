Privacy Policy

Australian Child Support Calculator

auschildsupport.com

Last updated: December 27, 2024

1. Overview

Australian Child Support Calculator ("we", "us", "our") operates a lead generation service that helps parents estimate child support outcomes and connect with Australian family law firms.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, store, and protect personal information.

Business details

  • Service name: Australian Child Support Calculator
  • Website: https://auschildsupport.com
  • Privacy contact: alex@auschildsupport.com

2. What personal information we collect

We collect personal information directly from users when they use our calculator, submit forms, or request a legal consultation.

Information we may collect includes:

Parent details
  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • State or territory of residence
Financial information
  • Income details (gross or approximate)
  • Employment status (if provided)
Family and child details
  • Number of children
  • Care arrangements
  • Relationship status
  • Child support related inputs used in calculations
Technical data
  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Pages visited and interaction data
  • Cookies and analytics data (see Section 9)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive information unless it is voluntarily provided and reasonably necessary for the service.

3. Why we collect this information

We collect personal information to:

  • Provide child support estimate calculations
  • Match users with suitable Australian family law firms
  • Forward leads to lawyers for consultation purposes
  • Improve calculator accuracy and user experience
  • Communicate with users about their enquiry
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary for these purposes, in line with APP 3.

4. How we use and share personal information

Use of information

We may use your personal information to:

  • Generate child support calculation results
  • Contact you regarding your enquiry
  • Assess suitability for referral to a lawyer
  • Improve our services and website functionality

Sharing information with lawyers

If you request a consultation or submit your details, we may disclose your information to independent Australian family law firms.

These firms:

  • Receive leads for consultation purposes
  • Are charged per lead
  • Operate under their own privacy policies
  • Are responsible for their own legal advice and services

We only share information that is relevant to the consultation request.

5. Third-party disclosure

We may disclose personal information to:

  • Family law firms and legal practitioners
  • IT service providers, hosting providers, and analytics providers
  • Professional advisers where required (legal, accounting)
  • Government authorities if required by law

We do not sell personal information to data brokers or unrelated third parties.

6. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

Security measures include:

  • Secure hosting environments
  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

No system is completely secure, but we take privacy protection seriously and regularly review our safeguards.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Meet legal, regulatory, and reporting obligations
  • Resolve disputes or enforce agreements

Typical retention periods:

  • Lead data: up to 24 months
  • Analytics data: aggregated and anonymised where possible

When information is no longer required, it is securely deleted or de-identified.

8. Your rights

Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal limits
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at alex@auschildsupport.com.

We will respond within a reasonable time, usually within 30 days.

9. Cookies and analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website performance
  • Understand how users interact with our calculator
  • Measure traffic and usage patterns

This may include third-party analytics tools such as Google Analytics.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

10. Cross-border disclosure

Some service providers, such as hosting or analytics providers, may store or process data outside Australia.

Where this occurs:

  • We take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients comply with privacy standards comparable to Australian law
  • Data is handled in accordance with APP 8
