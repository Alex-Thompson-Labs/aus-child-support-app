# Product Overview

Australian Child Support Calculator - a B2B lead generation platform connecting parents with family law practitioners.

## Business Model
- Parents use a **free calculator** implementing the official Services Australia 8-step formula
- **Complexity detection** identifies high-value cases (income variance, court dates, Change of Assessment reasons)
- Lawyers pay **$50 per qualified lead** via Pay-per-lead or Monthly Retainer models
- **Privacy First**: Leads delivered via Secure Magic Links (no PII in emails)

## Target Users
1. **Parents** - Navigating child support calculations, often in dispute scenarios
2. **Family Law Firms** - Seeking qualified leads for complex cases

## Current Phase
Phase 3A - Validation (Proof Before Pitch)
- Proving lead quality before scaling
- Target: 15%+ Lead-to-Consultation Rate or 1 Exclusive Partner signed

## Key Conversion Flow
1. Parent enters calculation details → instant results
2. Complexity triggers detect high-value cases automatically
3. SmartConversionFooter shows priority-based CTA (dispute risk > high value > binding agreement)
4. Inquiry form captures lead with calculation context
5. Admin reviews → generates Secure Magic Link → lawyer pays → accesses lead
