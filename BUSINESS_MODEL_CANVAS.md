# Business Model Canvas

## Customer Support Calculator (CSC)

### Key Partners
- Legal professionals and lawyers (for validation and referrals)
- Mental health organizations (potential partnerships)
- Family law associations
- Technology platform providers
- Payment processors

### Key Activities
- Child support calculation algorithm development and maintenance
- User experience design and optimization
- Legal compliance and accuracy verification
- Customer support and education
- Marketing and user acquisition
- Content creation (educational materials)

### Key Resources
- Child support calculation engine
- Web application infrastructure
- Legal expertise and consultation
- Brand and reputation
- User data and analytics
- Educational content library

### Value Propositions
- **Accuracy**: Precise child support calculations based on current legal guidelines
- **Accessibility**: Easy-to-use tool available 24/7
- **Transparency**: Clear breakdown of calculations and explanations
- **Affordability**: Lower cost alternative to immediate legal consultation
- **Empowerment**: Helps users understand their obligations/entitlements
- **Time-saving**: Quick results without scheduling appointments
- **Educational**: Provides guidance on the child support process

### Customer Relationships
- Self-service platform
- Educational content and guides
- Email support
- FAQ and knowledge base
- Lawyer referral pathway
- Follow-up communications for changes in circumstances

### Channels
- Website/web application
- Search engine optimization (SEO)
- Social media
- Family law websites and forums
- Legal professional referrals
- Content marketing (blog, guides)
- Paid advertising (Google Ads, social media)

### Customer Segments
- **Separating/divorcing parents**: Need to understand support obligations
- **Single parents**: Seeking to understand entitlements
- **Legal representatives**: Pre-screening tool for clients
- **Mediators**: Supporting negotiation processes
- **Policy researchers**: Analyzing support scenarios

### Cost Structure
- Technology infrastructure (hosting, development)
- Software development and maintenance
- Legal consultation and compliance
- Marketing and customer acquisition
- Customer support operations
- Business operations and administration
- Payment processing fees

### Revenue Streams
- **Freemium Model**:
  - Free basic calculations
  - Premium detailed reports and documentation
- **Lawyer Inquiry Fees**: Commission or referral fees from connected lawyers
- **Subscription Service**: Monthly access for users with changing circumstances
- **Professional Licenses**: For lawyers, mediators, and legal organizations
- **API Access**: For integration with other legal tech platforms
- **Educational Courses**: Online courses about child support and family law

---

## Notes

### Competitive Advantages
- User-friendly interface designed for non-lawyers
- Up-to-date with current legal requirements
- Clear explanations alongside calculations
- Seamless transition to legal professional support
- Educational focus builds trust

### Critical Success Factors
- Accuracy and legal compliance
- User trust and credibility
- SEO and discoverability
- Conversion rate optimization
- Strategic partnerships with legal professionals
