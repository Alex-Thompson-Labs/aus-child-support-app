## ‚ö†Ô∏è RISKS & MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### Business Risks

**Risk 1: Lead Quality Issues**
- **Impact:** High refund rate, lawyer churn
- **Mitigation:** Manual quality review, tighten complexity thresholds, lawyer feedback loop
- **Early Warning:** Refund rate >20%, NPS <0

**Risk 2: Insufficient Traffic**
- **Impact:** Not enough leads to sustain lawyer base
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive SEO, paid ads, content marketing, partnership with parent groups
- **Early Warning:** <500 visitors/month after 3 months

**Risk 3: Pricing Rejection**
- **Impact:** Can't sign enough lawyers
- **Mitigation:** Test multiple price points, offer trial periods, volume discounts
- **Early Warning:** <5 lawyers signed after 50 outreach attempts

**Risk 4: Lawyer Churn**
- **Impact:** Constant need to replace partners
- **Mitigation:** Regular check-ins, improve lead quality, flexible pricing, value-add services
- **Early Warning:** >15% monthly churn, NPS <0

### Technical Risks

**Risk 5: Database/Security Issue**
- **Impact:** Privacy breach, loss of trust, legal liability
- **Mitigation:** Regular security audits, proper RLS policies, data encryption, backup strategy
- **Early Warning:** Unusual database access patterns

**Risk 6: Web App Performance**
- **Impact:** Poor UX, low conversion, bad reputation
- **Mitigation:** Performance monitoring, regular testing, scalable infrastructure
- **Early Warning:** Lighthouse score <80, load time >3 seconds

### Regulatory Risks

**Risk 7: Privacy Act Compliance**
- **Impact:** Fines, legal action, business shutdown
- **Mitigation:** ‚úÖ Already compliant - privacy policy, consent, audit trails, deletion capability
- **Early Warning:** Privacy complaints, regulatory inquiries

**Risk 8: Legal Services Regulations**
- **Impact:** Classified as unlawful lead generation
- **Mitigation:** Clear disclaimers, no legal advice given, lawyers pay for introductions only
- **Early Warning:** Complaints from law societies

## üí° PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY

### Design Principles

**1. Parent First**
- Free calculator must genuinely help parents
- No dark patterns or manipulative upsells
- Clear, honest communication
- Respect for difficult life situations

**2. Quality Over Quantity**
- Would rather deliver 50 great leads than 200 mediocre ones
- Lawyers should love getting our leads
- High bar for what qualifies as "complex enough"

**3. Manual Before Automated**
- Prove value with manual processes first
- Automate only when volume demands it
- Don't over-engineer for problems we don't have yet

**4. Transparent & Ethical**
- Clear about how we make money
- No hidden fees or surprises
- Comply with spirit of regulations, not just letter

**5. Sustainable Business**
- Profitable unit economics from day one
- Don't grow faster than quality can sustain
- Long-term relationships over short-term revenue

### Feature Prioritization Framework

**For each proposed feature, ask:**

1. **Does it increase lead quality?** (Most important)
2. **Does it increase conversion rate?**
3. **Does it reduce manual work?**
4. **Does it improve lawyer satisfaction?**
5. **Does it help us scale?**

**Priority Scoring:**
- Must Have: Answers "yes" to #1 or required for compliance
- Should Have: Answers "yes" to #2 or #4
- Nice to Have: Answers "yes" to #3 or #5
- Won't Have: Answers "no" to all
