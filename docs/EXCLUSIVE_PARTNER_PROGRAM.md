### 1. The Marketing Retainer

### 2. Co-Branded Assets

- Unlike shared lead platforms, this program offers **exclusive rights** to the leads generated through the partner's co-branded instance.
- Includes automated intake summaries (incomes, care percentages) sent directly to the firm.
