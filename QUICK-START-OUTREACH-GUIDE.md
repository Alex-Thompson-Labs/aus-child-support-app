## ðŸ’¡ Quick Tips

### Email Best Practices:
- **Subject line**: Keep it clear and benefit-focused
- **First sentence**: State your purpose immediately
- **Body**: Focus on their audience's benefit, not your tool
- **Call to action**: Make it easy ("Would you consider adding this link?")
- **Signature**: Professional with contact info

### Phone Call Best Practices:
- **Be prepared**: Have talking points ready
- **Be respectful**: Ask if it's a good time
- **Be brief**: 2-3 minutes maximum
- **Get a name**: Ask who to follow up with
- **Send email**: Follow up with email after call

### Follow-Up Timing:
- **First follow-up**: 2 weeks after initial contact
- **Second follow-up**: 1 week after first follow-up
- **Give up**: After 2 follow-ups with no response
